About Me

I am an independent game developer, and under the name SilverSky Studio, I strive to create experiences that resonate deeply with players. My projects explore themes such as identity, loss, trauma, and new opportunities, inviting players to question their own morals and ethics through interactive storytelling.

My main focus is on visual novels and narrative-driven projects with visual elements, where each project is born from an aspect of the human mind and transformed into a space where players can feel, reflect, and fully immerse themselves in the experience. My goal with every project is to leave a lasting emotional impact.

What I do

  • Narrative design and scriptwriting
  • Artistic design of characters, environments, and settings
  • Programming in Ren'Py
